Hi! I'm Shadi

I am a creative technologist with 2 years of experience in software development and design with a BS in software engineering.

My enthusiasm for becoming a designer is driven by the desire to improve human-technology communication with the aim of transforming the world into a better and comfortable place for the current and future generations. I enjoy exploring the many ways through which our everyday lives can be improved through impactful solutions.

My interdisciplinary skill-set helps me to bridge the gap between design and development as well as the gap between the user and the product. Due to the rapid change in the world and society's problems, we need to provide the most applicable designs to meet this necessity.

In my spare time, I can often be found painting, running, dancing, or spending time with friends and family.
