Instagram Feature

In 2010, tapping a button to Instagram advent new revolution in social media that bringing you closer to the people and things you love across all over the world. Instagram has over one billion monthly active users and is one of the most popular social networks worldwide in 2019.

SKILLS: Heuristic evaluation, Interaction design, Prototyping, User flow diagramming

TOOLS: Figma, Photoshop

2019

Why red? Red is fast moving and promotes a need for action and movement.

Challenge

Have you ever want to categorize your following?

Have you ever just wanted to see the special post or story in special times of special users, you follow?... imagine it's late and you just came home from work and you open Instagram, absolutely you're not interested in "learning stuff" posts and neither "how to cook a good sushi" post! you just want to see some meme and humor or the photos of your friends hangouts.

I came up with a solution to address this problem.

Description

Many of the users follow hundreds of accounts including friends, family members, celebrities, favorite brands and businesses.

Lists allow users to organize the accounts you follow into categories and filter their feed based on the categories they have created.

Lists helps users easily access relevant information and lowers the chance of missing important content.

Switching between lists resembles switching between multiple accounts.

Further Considerations

  • Lists are private and only visible to the user.
  • Any account could be saved to more than one list.
  • Lists can be empty; users are not required to add accounts to the list upon creation
  • Detailed interactions such as renaming or deleting lists as wells as empty are included in the prototype.
