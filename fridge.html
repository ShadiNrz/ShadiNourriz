Future Fridge

Refrigerators have been around for 100 years and have functioned just fine, but we want to use technology for the highest interaction and impact. This is the story of how I made a positive difference in everyday life.

I tend to find ways to help people have superior performance with tasks related to food in their future homes with Augmented Reality technology.

SKILLS: User researcher, Contextual inquiry, Persona-based design, Prototyping, interaction design, Visual design

TOOLS: Adobe Xd, Adobe Dimension, Adobe illustrator

2020

Why yellow? Yellow is creative from a mental aspect, the color of new ideas

Challenge

What if we improve refrigerator user experience and help reduce food waste?

What if we create a healthy service based on a fridge besides save money and energy?

I came up with a solution to address this problem based on AR technology.

Empathy

We know the important role of refrigerator in our home. I observed that people have daily struggle with the food in their refrigerator. For example they tend to forget to consume the food in their refrigerator before the expiration date, and the food goes to waste, also they tired of checking regularly what they have in the refrigerator and what they need to buy.

I started the process with user interviews and talked with people about the way they use their fridge and organize food in their kitchen. I wanted to understand possible pain points and come up with requirements and features for the augmented smart fridge.

My discovery phase was limited to conducting some qualitative research, I choose to mix between user interview and observation (contextual inquiry) of how people use and behave with a product. I can better understand how the existing solutions work under real-life conditions and everyday situations.

Common answers of 5 people:

  • People waste some foods.
  • People forget what they have in the refrigerator.
  • People want to know the amount of food that they have.
  • People want to know more information about foods such as calories, fat,.. .
  • People forget about the place of their food.
  • People want to organize their food.
  • People want to have a needing list of their food.
  • People love to have some recipe according to their food.

Redifine

I collating data from the first stage to redefine the design problems and challenges.

I used personas constantly throughout the project to guide design decisions, priorities, and create empathy amongst the users.

Ideate

During this stage, I thought about different potential solutions to the research insights found previously. Some of these possible options offer a solution for different user concerns. ​ While I was mapping some ideas I came up with some basic tools the users would be able to utilize to achieve the goal and also reduced food waste:

  • To find what products they currently have in their fridge and the amount of each main food group (fruit, vegetables, dairy, protein, drinks, other).
  • To get information about products nutrients (calory,fat,cardohydrat,protein,salt,sugar).
  • To make a shopping list with sort in decreasing amount and be able to share with the rest of the family members.
  • To receive suggestions for meals/ recipes with the food currently in the fridge.
